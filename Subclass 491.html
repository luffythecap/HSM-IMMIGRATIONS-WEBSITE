Skilled Work Regional (Provisional) Visa (Subclass 491)

Your Pathway to Permanent Residency in Regional Australia

Get Expert Consultation

Quick Enquiry

Key Highlights of Subclass 491 Visa

Visa Duration & Benefits

- Valid for 5 years from the date of grant

- Permission to work and study in designated regional areas

- Access to Medicare and public healthcare

- Unlimited travel in and out of Australia

- Include family members in your application

Regional Commitment

- Live, work, and study in designated regional areas only

- Must live in the nominating region for the duration of visa

- Regional areas include all of Australia except:

• Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane metropolitan areas

• Gold Coast, Newcastle, Wollongong

Pathway to Permanent Residency

- Eligible for Subclass 191 permanent visa after:

• 3 years of regional residence

• Minimum taxable income requirement for 3 years

• Compliance with visa conditions

- Can include dependent family members in PR application

Detailed Eligibility Requirements

✓

Age Requirement:

- Must be under 45 years when invited

- Age is calculated at the time of invitation, not application

- No exceptions to the age requirement

✓

English Language Proficiency:

- Minimum Competent English required (IELTS 6.0 in each band or equivalent)

- Higher points for Superior English (IELTS 8.0 or equivalent)

- Accepted tests: IELTS, TOEFL iBT, PTE Academic, CAE

✓

Skills Assessment:

- Must have positive skills assessment in nominated occupation

- Occupation must be on relevant skilled occupation list

- Assessment must be valid at time of invitation

- Different assessing authorities for different occupations

✓

Nomination Requirements:

- Must be nominated by either:

• State/Territory government agency, OR

• Eligible family sponsor living in designated regional area

- Family sponsors must be Australian citizens or permanent residents

- Must meet specific state/territory nomination criteria if applicable

✓

Financial Requirements:

- Must have sufficient funds to settle in regional Australia

- Evidence of funds may be required by nominating state/territory

- Additional funds required for family members

Points System

To be eligible for the Subclass 491 visa, you must score at least 65 points. Points are awarded based on various factors:

Age (18-45 years) 30

Comprehensive Application Process

The application process for Subclass 491 visa involves multiple stages. Here's a detailed breakdown of each step:

1

Skills Assessment

Before starting your application:

  • Identify your occupation on the Skilled Occupation List
  • Contact relevant skills assessment authority
  • Prepare and submit required documents
  • Processing time: 1-4 months depending on authority
  • Validity: Usually 2-3 years
2

English Language Test

Choose from accepted tests:

  • IELTS
  • PTE Academic
  • TOEFL iBT
  • CAE

Results validity: 3 years from test date

3

Expression of Interest (EOI)

Submit through SkillSelect:

  • Create an ImmiAccount
  • Complete all sections accurately
  • Upload supporting documents
  • Validity: 2 years
  • Can be updated anytime
4

State/Territory Nomination

Apply for regional nomination:

  • Research state nomination criteria
  • Submit separate application to chosen state
  • Provide additional documentation if required
  • Pay nomination fees if applicable
  • Processing time: 2-12 weeks
5

Invitation to Apply

After receiving invitation:

  • Valid for 60 days only
  • Check all details are correct
  • Prepare visa application documents
  • Arrange health examinations
  • Obtain police certificates
6

Visa Application

Submit within 60 days:

  • Complete Form 47SK
  • Pay visa application charge
  • Upload all required documents
  • Provide biometrics if required
  • Processing time: 6-8 months

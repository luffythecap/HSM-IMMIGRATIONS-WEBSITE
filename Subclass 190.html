How to Get Australia Skilled Nominated Visa (Subclass 190)?

Subclass 190 allows skilled workers nominated by an Australian state or territory to live and work permanently in Australia.

Get Expert Consultation

Quick Enquiry

Key Features of Subclass 190 Visa

🌏

Permanent Residency

Live and work permanently in Australia with immediate access to Medicare and social security benefits.

📝

State Nomination

Gain additional 5 points through state/territory nomination, improving your chances of selection.

👥

Family Inclusion

Include your spouse, children and other eligible dependent relatives in your application.

Eligibility Criteria

✓

Be under 45 years of age at the time of application

✓

Have a positive skills assessment in your nominated occupation

✓

Your occupation must be on the relevant state's skilled occupation list

✓

Score at least 65 points on the points test

✓

Have at least competent English (IELTS 6.0 or equivalent)

✓

Meet the required skills qualifications and experience

✓

Receive a nomination from an Australian state or territory

Points Test Criteria

Factor Maximum Points
Age (25-32 years) 30 points
English language ability (Superior) 20 points
Skilled employment (8-10 years) 20 points
Educational qualifications (Doctorate) 20 points
Australian study requirement 5 points
Regional study 5 points
Professional year 5 points
State/Territory nomination 5 points

Application Process

1

Skills Assessment

Obtain a positive skills assessment from the relevant assessing authority

2

Submit EOI

Create and submit an Expression of Interest through SkillSelect

3

State Nomination

Apply for and receive nomination from a state or territory

4

Visa Application

Lodge your visa application with all required documents
